Project Culture

Meet The Band

Project Culture is an indie rock band from London that combines a melodic jangle-pop sound - indebted to bands like The Smiths, The Stone Roses, and The Velvet Underground - with frenzied and impassioned lyrics in the vein of Talking Heads, Arcade Fire, and Car Seat Headrest.

The band formed at Queen Mary University where singer and guitarist Gary Hill, out of frustration over the lack of a music scene on campus, started a music society that hosted live music nights at the university's student bar, and pubs in the area. The society helped create a close group of musicians, and through these friendships, he found the rest of the line-up.

Intending for 2020 to be a big year for them, the band plans to hit the ground running with the release of their debut single, the double a-side 'Character Actor/Guess I Think Too Much'. This will be the first of many releases this year, with the members being eager to demonstrate the vast range of genres present in their songs, as well as grow as a live band.

Gary Hill - Guitar and Vocals
Jamie Richardson - Bass
Peter Stanley - Guitar and Vocals
Toby Cashman - Drums
